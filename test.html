Specially Adapted Housing Grant complications...

I was awarded a SAH GRANT, not a loan, a few years ago, that I didn't need at the time as my house was previously built ADA compliant for my SC disabilities. The local VA O.T. reviewed my plans and made some suggestions for things I hadn't thought about, and they were incorporated into our plans before building. I've been living in the house for 12 years now, with no accessibility problems due to our careful planning.
